Hotel Lounge With Veranda

Classicism and Conservatism

The interior design was made for customers who love outdoor activities, hunting and fishing. The use of wood materials in furniture, tiles, paintings creates a feeling of connection with nature and natural comfort and coziness. All project management work was done online. We redesigned the flooring, replaced the doors, and used light shades of paint to expand the space of the rooms. The general design is made in a classic style. For the walls, we added textiles and painting according to the sketch of the designer. We also added decoration elements. This entire interior was created from scratch with the individual design project.
